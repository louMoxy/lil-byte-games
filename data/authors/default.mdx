Hi, I'm Louise.

I'm a frontend developer based in London — over ten years in the industry, which is simultaneously impressive and slightly alarming. By night I'm attempting to become an indie game developer, which is going about as smoothly as you'd expect.

I grew up loving games — quietly, on my own, because in the 90s girls weren't really supposed to. I loved them anyway. The problem solving, the worlds, the feeling of making progress at something. That never left me, even when life went in very different directions. _(I did a choreography degree, which while not usefulit does mean I am great on the dancefloor.)_

I've been learning Unity for about four years now. I've done too many courses, survived some game jams, and spent more evenings than I'd like to admit trying to code after a full day of already coding.
